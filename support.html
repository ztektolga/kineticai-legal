Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?

Credits are the currency used to generate AI content in Kinetic AI. Each feature uses a different amount of credits:

Video Generation200 Credits
Live Photo Animation120 Credits
Photo Editing50 Credits
Profile Photo20 Credits
Duo Merge Photo50 Credits
Virtual Try-On50 Credits

You can purchase credit packages from the in-app store. You also earn free credits daily through the Daily Spin feature.

I purchased credits but they didn't appear in my account

If your credits didn't appear after purchase, please try the following steps:

1. Close the app completely and reopen it.

2. Check your internet connection and try again.

3. Go to the credit purchase screen and tap "Restore Purchases".

4. If the issue persists, contact us at support@kineticai.studio with your Apple ID email and approximate purchase time. We will resolve it promptly.

How long does AI generation take?

Generation times vary by feature: Video generation typically takes 2-5 minutes. Photo editing and Live Photos usually complete within 30-90 seconds. You can close the app during generation - we'll send you a notification when your content is ready.

What photo formats are supported?

Kinetic AI supports JPEG, PNG, and HEIC photo formats. For best results, use clear, well-lit photos where faces are clearly visible and front-facing.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the app: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data including credits, generation history, and personal information. This action cannot be undone.

Can I get a refund for purchased credits?

Credit purchases are processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. For refund requests, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com (Apple) or Google Play's refund page. Kinetic AI does not process payments directly.

Why did my generation fail?

Generation can sometimes fail due to: low-quality or very small images, photos with no clear face visible, unstable internet connection, or high server demand. If generation fails, your credits are not deducted. Try using a different, clearer photo and ensure you have a stable connection.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your uploaded photos are used solely for AI processing and are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours. We do not use your photos to train AI models and never share them with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
