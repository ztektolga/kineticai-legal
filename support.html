Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?

Credits are used to generate AI content. Different features cost different amounts: Video generation uses 200 credits, Live Photos use 120 credits, Photo editing uses 50 credits, and Profile photos use 20 credits. You can purchase credit packages from within the app.

I purchased credits but they didn't appear

Please try the following: 1) Close and reopen the app. 2) Go to the purchase screen and tap "Restore Purchases". 3) Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, contact us with your Apple ID email and we'll resolve it promptly.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data including credits, generation history, and personal information. This action cannot be undone.

What photo formats are supported?

Kinetic AI supports JPEG, PNG, and HEIC photo formats. For best results, use clear, well-lit photos where faces are clearly visible.

Can I get a refund?

Credit purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. For refund requests, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly.
