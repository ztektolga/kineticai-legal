Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Data We Collect

At Kinetic AI ("we", "our", "us"), we value your privacy. We collect the following data:

  • Account information (email address, name — when provided via Apple/Google Sign-In)
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Usage statistics (anonymous)
  • Purchase history (in-app purchases)
  • Photos and videos you upload (for AI processing only)
  • Push notification tokens (if notifications are enabled)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Providing AI photo and video processing services
  • Managing your account and credit balance
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Sending push notifications about completed generations
  • Offering customer support
  • Fulfilling legal obligations

3. Data Security

  • Your data is protected with industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL).
  • Uploaded media files are processed and deleted from our servers within 24 hours.
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text.
  • Authentication tokens (JWT) are used for secure session management.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for specific functionality:

  • Fal.ai — AI model processing (images and videos are sent for processing and deleted after completion)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — Payment processing for in-app purchases
  • DigitalOcean — Cloud infrastructure and file storage
  • Expo — Push notification delivery

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing their handling of data.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete this data immediately. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@kineticai.app.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (available in-app via Settings > Delete Account)
  • Objection: Object to specific data processing activities
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, email privacy@kineticai.app

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Uploaded media is deleted within 24 hours of processing.
  • Generation history is retained until you delete it or delete your account.
  • When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies within the App. Only basic, anonymous analytics data is collected to improve app performance. We do not use any third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and on this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
