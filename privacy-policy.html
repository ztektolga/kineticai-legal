Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Kinetic AI handles your data.

Last updated: March 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Kinetic AI, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and authentication credentials when you sign up via Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In.
  • Photos and Videos: Images and videos you upload for AI processing. These are temporarily stored on our servers for processing and are automatically deleted after generation is complete.
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, feature interactions, and crash reports to improve our service.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and support purposes.
  • Purchase Information: Transaction records for in-app purchases processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered photo and video generation services.
  • To process and verify in-app purchases and manage your credit balance.
  • To send you notifications about your generation status (if enabled).
  • To improve our AI models and app performance (using anonymized, aggregated data only).
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

3. Photo and Video Processing

Photos and videos you upload are sent to our secure servers for AI processing. We want you to know:

  • Your uploaded media is used solely for generating your requested content.
  • Uploaded files are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours after processing.
  • Generated content (results) are stored temporarily so you can download them, and are deleted within 7 days.
  • We do not use your photos or videos to train AI models.
  • We do not share your photos or videos with any third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All data transmissions are encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols.
  • User data is stored on secure servers with restricted access.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of data.
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple and Google; we never store your payment card information.

5. Third-Party Services

Kinetic AI uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In: For secure authentication.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: For processing in-app purchases.
  • AI Processing APIs: For generating photos and videos (your media is sent securely to these services for processing only).
  • Expo Push Notifications: For delivering generation status notifications.

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view your account information within the app's Settings page.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and irreversible.
  • Data Portability: You can download your generated content from the app before deleting your account.
  • Opt-out: You can disable notifications at any time from the app settings or your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Kinetic AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete such data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
